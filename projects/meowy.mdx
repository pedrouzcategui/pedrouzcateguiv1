# Meowy

Meowy is an innovative robotic companion designed to provide entertainment and assistance to cat owners. This interactive robot combines hardware programming with 3D design to create a lifelike feline friend that can play with real cats, dispense treats, and even learn from user interactions.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Play**: Equipped with sensors to detect and respond to cat movements.
- **Treat Dispenser**: Automated feeding system with customizable schedules.
- **Learning Algorithm**: Adapts to your cat's preferences over time.
- **Remote Control**: Monitor and control via a mobile app.

## Hardware Design

The robot's chassis was designed in Blender, featuring:
- Arduino-based microcontroller for real-time processing.
- Custom C++ firmware for efficient operation.
- Linux-based companion app for advanced features.
- Durable materials suitable for pet interaction.

Meowy represents the intersection of robotics, AI, and pet care, offering a unique solution for busy pet owners who want to ensure their cats stay happy and engaged.
